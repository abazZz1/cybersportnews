About CS:GO

Global Offensive has one of the most popular esport scenes in the world.The Global Offensive professional scene consists of leagues and tournaments hosted by third-party organisations, and Valve-sponsored tournaments known as Major Championships. Majors are considered the most prestigious tournaments in Counter-Strike circuit and have among the largest prize pools; originally announced at $250,000, the prize pools for Majors have risen to US$1,000,000 since MLG Columbus 2016. Astralis is the most successful Global Offensive team of all time, with the core members of that team winning four Majors together.
